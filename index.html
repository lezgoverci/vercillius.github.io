What will this Program do for you?

This exclusively-designed Executive MasterClass In Digital Transformation is one of the innovative and highly relevant programs developed under the educational partnership between UnionBank and Global Learning Solutions Singapore.
It addresses the urgent need of today’s organizations to become agile and adaptive in the midst of the digital disruptions that characterize the 4th Industrial Revolution where Cyber and Physical Realities merge into one.

Course Objectives

1

Analyse the pervasive influence of Disruptive Exponential Technologies in the way we work, live, learn, communicate, engage, entertain and consume goods and services

2

Understand the process of Customer Value Creation and the importance of Ecommerce and social media marketing to increase customer engagement, intimacy and loyalty

3

Know the difference between Digitalization and Digital Transformation and learn the process of delivering seamless digital experience and solutions and become digital to the core

4

Appreciate the importance of creating an Agile and Lean Culture to develop future-ready digital competencies, embed digital in the fabric of the enterprise and move from doing digital to being digital

5

Understand the pillars of Technology Enterprise Architecture and the importance of embedding IT within the business, managing data privacy and launching proactive cybersecurity measures

6

Develop an Intrapreneurial Mindset to lead digital transformation efforts by embracing the perspective of a startup founder and applying design thinking to continuously introduce innovative ideas

7

Embrace Transformational Leadership as the core strategy to drive digital transformation by mastering the disciplines of execution and developing a people-centric transformation strategy

8

Create a Culture of Innovation across the organization to sustain digital transformation by overcoming innovation fatigue and promoting open innovation policies and practices at all levels

Three Factors That Make This Course Unique

SPECIALLY CURATED CURRICULUM CONTENT

Expertly designed to create an education system that combines rigorous academic certification with work-related project assignments and mentor assessments. Pre-readings, case studies, group discussions, back-home assignments and final consulting project are meticulously incorporated.

PRACTICE-ORIENTED FACULTY MENTORS

Delivered by Global Faculty Mentors who are industry experts, academic thought leaders and active business founders who have hands- on experiences in multiple industries. They bring global best practices in such areas as FinTech, AI, IOT, Blockchain, Analytics and Open Innovation.

IMMERSIVE VIRTUAL LEARNING DELIVERY

Incorporating synchronous learning (faculty and students meeting virtually at the same time) and asynchronous learn- ing (students accessing online content at their own pace and time) to engage students in their own learning process. Live sessions with collaborative discussions and mentoring.
